Quest of the Dragon's Eye

As you walk through the fields you are worried, if not a bit concerened that there are no animals; no cows, sheep not even birds any were to be seen.
You carry on the grass gets taller, first it goes up to your ankles, then your knees, then your waist and then your head and over it.
Jumping up to see any clearer patches ahead you see a mound of grass rising up from the ground, it's only a few meters ahead, mabye 20.
As you walk up to the top of it you realise there is a small, round wooden door cut into the side of the hill you climb to the very top and look ahead.
Everywere you look there is the tall grass and the flat ground with no life apart from you in it. You decide to have a little peek, mabye a little adventure
Anyways it can't be worse that what you have already been thorugh. you pull the handle and peer inside, a set of spiraling staircases going down.
There is just enough space for you to squeeze down.
You go down plunging yourself into the cold, tight space that is a hole in the side of a hill.
